About The Game

The Last Stand of the Worms is a game where you play as a worm while you try to survive in the wild. Survive as long as you can while getting stronger and faster, the only downside the longer you survive the stronger the enemies get. Unlock stronger allies by surviving, they shall help you defeat your enemies and win the battle! So join The Last Stand Of The Worms!!
